Indomie Noodles

A Quick and Delicious meal prepared using Indomie noodles

Ingredients

  • Indomie Noodles
  • Cooling Oil
  • Meat(Any Meat of Choice)
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil water and add your noodles
  2. Heat up your cooking oil and add all your sliced Vegetables of choice
  3. Add up spices to taste
  4. Pour in your cooking noodles and stir to mix
