Rice Aroni

This is a special menu prepared by people living in west africa, Ghana presisely

Rice Aroni is easy to prepare, and can be served at any event. In Ghana here, it is served on special occasions like weddings, graduations etc..

The Special ingredients are as follows: Rice, Tin Tomatoes, Fresh Tomatoes,Onions,Garlic, Ginger,and any soecial spices of choice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Spaghetti
  • Cooling Oil
  • Meat(Any Meat of Choice)
  • Tin Tomatoes
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (Any spices of choice

Steps

  1. Heat up your cooking oil
  2. Add your sliced onions and let it fry till its brownish
  3. Add your Tin Tomatoes and fry for like 5 mins
  4. Add your blended Fresh Tomatoes, Garlic,Ginger and let it fry for extra 5 mins
  5. Add your preferred spices and pour in your steamed Meat
  6. Pour water appropraite to cook the rice to want
  7. Rice the Rice and pour into the stew on fire
  8. Add Salt to taste
  9. Stir the content periodically till the Rice becomes soft to your satisfaction
